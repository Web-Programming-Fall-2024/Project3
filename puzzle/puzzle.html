How to Play my Fifteen Puzzle

The Fifteen Puzzle (also known as a Sliding Puzzle) is a timeless classic that challenges your logic and patience. The game consists of a 4x4 grid of numbered tiles, with one square left empty.

Your goal is simple but tricky:

  1. Rearrange the tiles so that they are in numerical order from 1 to 15, with the empty square at the bottom-right corner.
  2. To move a tile, slide any tile adjacent to the empty square into the empty space.
  3. Keep sliding tiles until the numbers are arranged correctly!
